How it works

Buy the token and a Hash appears, drawn on-chain

You buy $HASH on Uniswap the way you would any coin, and once you hold 1 whole token a living Hash NFT mints itself straight to your wallet — 24×24 SVG art and all. Every Hash is drawn by contracts on Ethereum, with no server, no IPFS, and no mint page to visit.

01

Buy $HASH

You swap ETH for $HASH on Uniswap the same way you would buy any other coin, with no mint button and no separate app to deal with.

02

Hold 1, get a Hash

Hold 1 whole $HASH and a living Hash appears in your wallet. Each additional whole token spawns another. Drop below 1 and the NFT burns automatically.

03

Claim your share

Each Hash accrues protocol fees from the 4% swap tax. Rewards build in the background and you claim them whenever you want — no staking, no lockups.

The Hash

A living NFT, drawn fully on-chain

Every Hash is drawn by contracts on Ethereum rather than a server, and its look is read from the chain, so it shifts as you hold and seal it. Sealing one locks it for good and preserves the frozen tokenURI permanently on-chain.
